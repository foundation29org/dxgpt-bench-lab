Analiza la similitud semántica entre diagnósticos diferenciales generados (DDX) y diagnósticos de referencia (GDX). Para cada par DDX-GDX, proporciona un score del 1-10 donde:

1-20: Unrelated - Different concepts/conditions with no meaningful connection
21-40: Loosely related - Share some domain overlap but are distinct diagnoses
41-60: Family relationship - GDX is a subset or member of the broader DDX category
61-80: Near match - Synonyms, or minor terminology variations
81-100: Enhanced match - Either exact match or DDX provides more specific, detailed, or expert-level terminology than GDX

Express scores as [0, 1]

Para cada item, encuentra el GDX con mayor similitud al DDX y retorna ese par con su score.
